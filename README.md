## <img src="https://skillicons.dev/icons?i=vscode" width="28"/> About Me

I'm an AI/ML engineering student at **Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education (KARE)**, focused on building practical, production-minded systems rather than isolated coursework exercises. My interests sit at the intersection of **applied machine learning, automation engineering, and full-stack fundamentals** — I like understanding how a system behaves end-to-end, not just the model layer.

I approach AI/ML with a **verification-first mindset**: I've published research documenting a case where a large language model produced an incomplete mathematical solution, catching the error by cross-checking against a standard reference text. That instinct — question the output, verify against ground truth — shapes how I build and evaluate everything I ship.

Alongside coursework, I'm building **AI-driven automation workflows** using self-hosted n8n, and developing a personal engineering brand through writing, open documentation, and public project work.
